    Selection

    There are two ways to select nodes and branches in Spline Plus R. The first method is to click directly on a node, which will also select the associated branches. To select multiple nodes, hold the Ctrl key while clicking.


    The second method is to use rectangular selection. Click and drag in your Unity 3D scene view to draw a rectangle; any nodes within this rectangle, along with their associated branches, will be selected. To select multiple nodes using rectangular selection, hold the Ctrl key while dragging to include additional nodes


    Deselection

    To deselect all selected nodes and branches, click anywhere in your scene view or press Esc on your keyboard. This will clear your selection and set you back to selection mode.

    To deselect a specific node or branch from the selection, simply click on the node or branch again to remove it from your selection list.
